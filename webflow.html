Critical Power

Monitor your BESS

Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) are crucial in our energy transition. With the net-zero ambition of harnessing 98% of newly generated power from renewable sources, the demand for BESS is increasing. This highlights the importance of the precautions we need to take to achieve grid reliability and stable energy output. Having secure and reliable Industrial Networks ensures 24/7 monitoring of all critical systems and protects your investment. Together with our long-term partner, Moxa - a global leader in industrial connectivity - Neteon offers cutting-edge IIoT solutions for your BESS deployments to monitor and optimize your operations.

Learn More

Power Quality & Consumption Monitoring

Power devices deployed near primary power sources, such as switchgear, automated transfer switches (ATS), and uninterrupted power supply (UPS), need continuous monitoring to maintain acceptable power quality. Neteon solely distributes Moxa, the leading manufacturer of Industrial IoT hardware, to ensure power levels can be observed 24/7. For businesses aiming to improve power efficiency, monitoring the operational status of their power distribution unit (PDU) provides valuable insights into daily power usage. Neteon, powered by Moxa IIoT hardware and solutions, ensures reliable connectivity for accurate transmission of power usage data.

Making Critical Power more Manageable

Critical power management solutions must ensure a reliable power supply to essential power loads, where no power shortages can be tolerated. This includes hospitals, data centers, and other industrial facilities among others. With our partner, Moxa, we ensure an uninterrupted power supply by utilizing automatic transfer switches (ATS), meters, circuit breakers, and UPS controllers to monitor power systems while optimizing energy loads.
